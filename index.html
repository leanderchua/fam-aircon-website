About Us

Engineered for Reliability.

At FAM Airconditioning Supply, we don't just sell equipment — we deliver climate control infrastructure. With over a decade of specialized experience in high-capacity HVAC systems, our team of certified engineers ensures every installation meets rigorous industrial standards.

Our commitment to excellence spans from initial load calculation and system design to meticulous installation and rigorous preventive maintenance. We partner with top-tier manufacturers — Carrier, Mitsubishi Electric, and Daikin — to provide robust, energy-efficient cooling solutions.
